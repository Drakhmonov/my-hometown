Top 3 things to do in Samarkand

Walk in the dream garden.
Boulevard is a peaceful avenue lined with tall trees and surrounded by historic universities — a favorite spot for locals to relax and enjoy the charm of Samarkand.
Discover the Blue Paradise.
Shohi Zinda is a sacred necropolis famous for its bright blue tiles and stunning Islamic architecture from the 14th century.
Taste the local life.
Siyob Bazaar is the heart of Samarkand’s daily life, where you can find spices, fruits, and traditional Uzbek sweets full of color and aroma.
